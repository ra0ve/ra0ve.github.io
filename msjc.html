Mt. San Jacinto College

I attended Mt. San Jacinto College from 2015 to 2019 and earned five associate degrees, including two Associate of Science degrees for transfer in Mathematics and Computer Science. My time here wasn’t just about knocking out classes—it was about digging deep into problem-solving, programming, and critical thinking across multiple disciplines.

The path wasn’t flawless—I repeated classes like Pre-Calculus and faced setbacks in chemistry and calculus—but I kept pushing. By the end of my time at MSJC, I had taken over 100 transferable units and earned a spot on the President’s Honor List during my final semester. That last stretch included acing courses in Linear Algebra, Calculus III, Physics, and Discrete Structures—all while pulling a perfect 4.0 GPA for the term.

MSJC gave me a foundation not only in academics but in learning how to learn, especially when things didn’t go smoothly the first time. It’s where I found out that finishing strong matters more than starting perfect.
